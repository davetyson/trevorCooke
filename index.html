About Trevor

I'm Trevor Cooke, and I’m an audio engineer located in Hamilton Ontario. Music has always been a huge part of my life, I’ve been playing in bands since I was 14. Writing, recording and gigging have been a constant since. Over the pandemic I became enamoured by recording, creating home projects and mixing them on a laptop. Over the past few years I’ve honed my craft and started working in venues and professional recording studios, turning my hobby into a living.

Today, I mainly work out of Threshold studios in Hamilton, Ontario, but am willing to travel and offer rates that will satisfy most budgets. From creating radio ready pop records, to the more indie, DIY-punk, and hardcore projects of my youth, I’m game to make records whenever and wherever I am needed.
